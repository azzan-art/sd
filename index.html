Where you'll stay

Beachfront Cottages

Native-style cottages sit right on the sand, a few steps from the water. They're the spot for daytime lounging and sunset watching — shady, breezy, and built for big groups to gather.

  • Steps from the shoreline
  • Shaded seating for the whole barkada
  • Perfect base for grilling & swimming days
